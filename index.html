UMA CAFE

This is an unofficial website for a coffee club in University of Mannheim NLP Group. Checkout our list of coffee equipments here and also a list of our favorite cafes in Mannheim. This website is mainly maintained by Sotaro who travels to visit cafes in Europe. Other members include Tommaso, an Italian who quickly rejected Moka and embraced V60, and Pedro, coffee-supervisor who dragged us into this deep coffee abyss.
